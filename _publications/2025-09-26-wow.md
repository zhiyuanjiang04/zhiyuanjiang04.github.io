Humans develop an understanding of intuitive physics through active interaction with the world. This motivates our central hypothesis: authentic physical intuition in a world model must be grounded in extensive, causally rich interactions with the real world. 

We introduce WoW, a 14B-parameter generative world model trained on 2 million real-world robot interaction trajectories. We establish WoWBench, a new benchmark focused on physical consistency and causal reasoning in video, where WoW achieves state-of-the-art performance.
